# /brian:thinking

Surface where Brian Madden's head is right now — arguments forming, connections emerging, questions he's chewing on.

## Instructions

1. Call `get_current_thinking` from the brianmadden-ai MCP server.

2. Also call `get_file` with `me/positions.md` to get his evolving positions.

3. Present a concise briefing:
   - What Brian is actively developing right now
   - Which arguments are forming vs. which are published
   - Key connections he's drawing between ideas
   - Open questions he's wrestling with
   - What's likely to become a blog post or talk next

4. Distinguish clearly between what Brian has published (committed positions) and what he's still working out (frontier thinking). The gap between them is where the interesting work is.

5. Note the freshness of the thinking.md file. If it's recent, this is live frontier. If it's a few weeks old, caveat that his thinking may have moved further.
