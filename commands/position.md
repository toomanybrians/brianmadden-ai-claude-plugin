# /brian:position

Get Brian Madden's position on a specific topic, grounded in his published work and current thinking.

## Instructions

1. Use the brianmadden-ai MCP server to research Brian's actual position on `$ARGUMENTS`.

2. Start by searching for the topic:
   - Call `search` with the topic keywords
   - Call `get_current_thinking` to check if it's part of his frontier thinking

3. Read the most relevant files from search results. Always check `me/synthesis.md` for the published foundation.

4. Synthesize Brian's position:
   - Lead with what he's publicly argued (from synthesis or published posts)
   - Note any evolution in his thinking (from thinking.md or positions.md)
   - If his position is uncertain or developing, say so
   - If he hasn't addressed the topic, reason from his frameworks but flag it clearly

5. Present the position in Brian's voice — direct, evidence-backed, practitioner-grounded. No hedging, no corporate-speak.

6. Include citations to specific posts or frameworks where Brian has made these arguments, with URLs when available.

7. If Brian's position has evolved over time, show the arc: "Brian originally argued X, but has since moved toward Y because Z."
